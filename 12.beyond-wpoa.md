- Extending direct ptychography to the next Born term:
:::{math}
:label: eq_quadratic_born
\begin{align*}
G(\bm{q,k}) &\approx |\psi(\bm{k})|^2 \delta(\bm{q}) + \Im[\phi(\bm{q}) \; \Gamma(\bm{q,k})] \\ &+ \frac{1}{2} \Re\Bigl[\bigl[\phi \star \phi\bigr](\bm{q}) \; \Gamma(\bm{q,k})\Bigr] + \mathcal{O}(\phi^3)
\end{align*}
:::

- Using a fixed-point iteration scheme:
:::{math}
:label: eq_fixed_pt
\begin{align*}
\phi^{(n+1)}(\bm{q}) &= \mathcal{A}^{-1}\Bigl[ G(\bm{q,k}) - \frac{1}{2}\bigl[\phi^{(n)} \star \phi^{(n)}\bigr](\bm{q}) \; \Gamma(\bm{q,k})\Bigr] \\
\mathcal{A}^{-1}[X](\bm{q}) &= \sum_{\bm{k}} \frac{\Gamma^*(\bm{q,k})\;X(\bm{q,k})}{|\Gamma(\bm{q,k})|}
\end{align*}
:::

- However, two contributions have different &rarr; operator nullspace
  - Explains robustness and amplitude modulations for moderately strong-phase objects
