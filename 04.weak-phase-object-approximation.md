:::::{tab-set}

::::{tab-item} Born series expansion
- Under the weak phase-object approximation (WPOA)
:::{math}
:label: eq_wpoa
\psi_{\mathrm{exit}}(\bm{r}) \approx 1 + \mathrm{i} \sigma V_p(\bm{r}) + \mathcal{O}(\sigma^2 V_p^2(\bm{r}))
:::

- The Fourier-transformed diffraction intensity is [@https://doi.org/cm9m8z]
:::{math}
:label: eq_forward_model
\begin{align*}
G(\bm{q,k}) &= |\psi(\bm{k})|^2 \delta(
\bm{q}) + \Gamma(\bm{q,k}) \phi(\bm{q}) \\
\Gamma(\bm{q,k}) &= \psi^*(\bm{k})\psi(\bm{k-q}) - \psi(\bm{k}) \psi^*(\bm{k+q})
\end{align*}
:::

::::

::::{tab-item} Transfer of information
- Measured intensity is linear in sample phase $\phi(\bm{q})$
  - Contrast transfer function (CTF) [@https://doi.org/chsgqd]
:::{math}
:label: eq_ctf
\begin{align*}
\Im\Bigl\{\mathrm{CTF}_j(\bm{q})\Bigr\} &= \frac{1}{2} \Bigl\{ \left[\psi \star \psi D_j \right](\bm{q}) - \left[\psi D_j \star \psi \right](\bm{q}) \Bigr\} \\
\psi(\bm{k}) &= A(\bm{k}) \mathrm{e}^{-\mathrm{i} \chi(\bm{k})}
\end{align*}
:::
