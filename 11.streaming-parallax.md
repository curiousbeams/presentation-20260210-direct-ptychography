- Direct ptychography algorithms can be formulated using incremental updates
  - Leveraging the linearity of convolutions and Fourier transforms
  - Enabling real-time streaming reconstructions, for scan index $j$ and BF pixel $m$

:::{math}
\begin{align*}
\phi_{\mathrm{prlx}}(\bm{r}) +&= I_j(m) \delta(\bm{r} - \bm{s}_m) \\
\phi_{\mathrm{prlx-flip}}(\bm{r})  +&= I_j(m) \mathcal{L}(\bm{r} - \bm{s}_m) \\
\mathcal{L}(\bm{r}) &= \mathcal{F}^{-1}\{\mathrm{sgn}[\sin[\chi(\bm{q})]]\}
\end{align*}

:::
